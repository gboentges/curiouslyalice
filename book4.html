Alicia en el País de las Maravillas
Illustrated by Júlia Sardà.

Rating: 4/5

This is a book I was very excited to get because I love the illustrator and look at the cover! It is inspired on the real Alice Liddell! While I absolutely adored the illustrations in this book, I was a little disappointed that there has hardly any interaction between the drawings and the story. Even the text itself was merely OK in parts like the Mouse Tale in terms of typography. The book is still enjoyable and the design has it charms, but I guess I expected more because I wanted it to stand out from the other books. I guess I wanted too much?
