About Me

Who is
Lewis Carroll?

Lewis Carroll, A.K.A. Charles Lutwidge Dodgson, is a world renowned photographer and author of Alice's Adventures in Wonderland.
He was also a Reverend at Christ Church, Oxford and a teacher in Mathematical Logic and Geometry.

About Me

What is
Wonderland?

Alice's Adventures in Wonderland was published in November 26, 1865. It revolutionized children's literature, and continues to enchant our minds even today. It has been translated to 172 languages, and adapted into more than 50 movies worldwide.

About Me

Hello, my name is Ella.

I am a computer scientist, day dreamer, illustrator, reader and big fan of Alice. I am a person who has always been in their own world, so I feel a big connection to Alice and her adventures. I believe imagination and creativity is as important as science and mathematics, so I like to be involved in both.

I am also a member of the Lewis Carroll Society of North America and part of the Discord Carrollian Group where we get to discuss, stream and share everything related to Alice, Lewis Carroll and everything in between.
