Alice in Wonderland

Rating: 5/5

This is a WONDERFUL book! I really have no complains about it. The pacing and the flow of the story in graphic novel form is perfect. The illustrations are beautiful, charming, colorful and fitting for the characters. Alice is perfectly portrayed as witty, confused, and curious as I always thought her to be. I enjoyed every single page of this novel. I believe is one of the best yet of Alice in Wonderland that I have read. Even if its a retold story, I think they did a great job of keeping the soul of the original. I truly believe Carroll would have approved of this book.
